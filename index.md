# VyraAI Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

## 1. Information We Collect

VyraAI collects only the information you choose to provide, including:

* Food logs and nutrition data
* Workout plans and exercise logs
* Profile information (age, weight, goals)
* Pantry and meal data

## 2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

* Provide personalized fitness and nutrition insights
* Improve app functionality
* Store your progress locally on your device

## 3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device.
VyraAI does not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

## 4. Health Disclaimer

VyraAI provides general fitness and nutrition guidance only.
It does not provide medical advice.

## 5. Third-Party Services

Some features (such as barcode scanning or AI responses) may use third-party services, but no personal data is sold.

## 6. Your Rights

You can:

* Delete all your data at any time from the app
* Control what information you provide
